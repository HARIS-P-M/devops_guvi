About the Program

Transform Your Career with DevOps

A comprehensive training program designed in partnership with HCL and GUVI to equip Kongu Engineering College students with cutting-edge DevOps skills and industry experience.

This intensive 8-week program combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical sessions, real-world projects, and mentorship from industry experts. Students will gain proficiency in modern DevOps tools, cloud platforms, and automation techniques essential for today's tech industry.
